## Understanding the Challenge

{{CLIENT_NAME}} is deploying or planning to deploy AI capabilities
where governance has not kept pace with adoption: {{SPECIFIC_CONTEXT,
e.g. "no named owner per use case," "no consistent risk evaluation
across use cases," "policy exists but is not operational"}}. The gap
is rarely the framework; it is translating an AI governance intent
into ownership, evidence and an operating rhythm that holds up under
board and regulatory scrutiny.

## Proposed Approach

Run through OPERA, the five-stage method behind every AI for U&I
engagement:

- **Opportunity** — catalogue current and planned AI use cases against
  business intent, ahead of technical capability
- **People** — assign a named owner for each use case and each
  governance decision
- **Evaluation** — apply a consistent risk model across use cases,
  calibrated to impact and reversibility
- **Response** — define escalation paths, approval gates and the
  decision record
- **Assurance** — establish the ongoing evidence trail and reporting
  cadence
