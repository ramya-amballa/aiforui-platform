## Proposed Approach

Run through OPERA, adapted to a governance operating model redesign:

- **Opportunity** — diagnose where governance debt has accumulated and
  which decisions actually matter
- **People** — redesign around decision rights first, then reporting
  lines, not the reverse
- **Evaluation** — sequence the transformation so the highest-risk
  gaps close early
- **Response** — establish the target operating model and the metrics
  that show whether it is actually being used
- **Assurance** — a reporting cadence that demonstrates value early
  enough to sustain support for what follows
