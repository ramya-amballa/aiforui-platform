# KPI Dashboard — Daily Brief

## Inputs

The daily output of all eight other AI employees, with CEO Advisor's
recommendation as the lead item.

## Outputs

`daily-revenue-brief-template.md`, filled in for the day.

## Success Metrics

- Brief generated every day, even in partial form, by 08:00
- Percentage of sections populated (vs. marked "not available today")
- Time from last upstream employee finishing to brief delivery

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Briefs generated on schedule | 7/7 days |
| Sections populated (not "not available") | 90%+ |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Briefs generated on schedule | 100% |
| Founder-reported "brief was actionable today" | Tracked qualitatively, reviewed monthly |

## Business Impact

Daily Brief is the practice's single interface to the whole operating
system. If it's unreliable, the founder stops trusting it and reverts
to checking every employee's raw output individually, which defeats
the purpose of building AOS in the first place.
