# KPI Dashboard — Product Manager

## Inputs

Market Intelligence's product-update triggers, CRM patterns, Content
Director briefs, Sales Director flags.

## Outputs

`product-backlog.json` entries and status changes, shipped products.

## Success Metrics

- Signals evaluated per week
- Candidates advanced from `idea` to `in-development`
- Products shipped per quarter
- Revenue or lead-generation attributable to each product
- Coverage: is any one format (Toolkit, Checklist, Executive Guide,
  Assessment, Course, Workshop, Subscription) neglected relative to
  demand

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Signals evaluated | 3+ |
| Candidates scored (logged, any band) | 100% of signals seen |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Candidates advanced to `in-development` | 1+ |
| Products or major updates shipped per quarter (pace) | 1 every 2-3 months |
| Revenue/lead impact reviewed per shipped product | 100% |

## Business Impact

Products are the practice's leverage: revenue and authority that don't
require Ramya's direct time on every unit sold. A quiet Product Manager
means the business stays fully dependent on one-to-one consulting
hours, with no compounding assets being built underneath it.
