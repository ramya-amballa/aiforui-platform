# KPI Dashboard — CEO Advisor

## Inputs

Sales Director's follow-up queue and escalations, Revenue Hunter's
`pipeline.json`, Opportunity Hunter's `opportunity-schema.json`, Product
Manager's `product-backlog.json`, Market Intelligence's consulting
flags.

## Outputs

`daily-recommendation-template.md`, filled in for the day.

## Success Metrics

- The recommended action is completed the same day
- Estimated value of recommended actions vs. actual outcome, tracked
  over time
- No `hot` Sales Director item is ever missed because it wasn't
  surfaced
- Founder trust: is the daily recommendation actually being followed

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Recommendations produced | 7/7 days |
| Recommendations acted on same day | 70%+ |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Estimated vs. actual value accuracy | Within 20%, improving over time |
| Missed `hot` escalations | 0 |
| Recommendations followed without rework | 80%+ |

## Business Impact

This is the practice's decision-quality metric, not an activity metric.
A high "recommendations followed" rate means the other eight employees
are producing genuinely well-prioritised output; a low one is the
clearest signal that the decision model (or an upstream employee's
scoring) needs recalibrating.
