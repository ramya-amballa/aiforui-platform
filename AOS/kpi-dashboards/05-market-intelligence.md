# KPI Dashboard — Market Intelligence

## Inputs

EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, DORA, GDPR, CBUAE AI Principles, RBI
AI guidance, AI security news.

## Outputs

`regulatory-log.json` entries; triggered LinkedIn/newsletter ideas,
product-update flags and consulting-opportunity flags.

## Success Metrics

- Time from publication to being logged
- Percentage of substantive developments that produced all four
  triggers
- Number of consulting opportunities traceable to a specific
  regulatory development
- Zero missed developments in a tracked source, checked retrospectively

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Source checks completed | 100% of tracked sources, every day |
| Substantive developments producing all 4 triggers | 100% |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Time from publication to logged | Under 24 hours, 90%+ of the time |
| Consulting opportunities traced to a regulatory development | 1+ |

## Business Impact

This is the practice's early-warning radar. AI for U&I's differentiator
is reacting to real regulatory change faster and more specifically than
generic commentary — a missed or late-logged development is a missed
first-mover advantage on content, product and outreach alike.
