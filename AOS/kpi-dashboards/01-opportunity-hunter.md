# KPI Dashboard — Opportunity Hunter

## Inputs

LinkedIn, Upwork and comparable platforms, tender/procurement portals,
conference CFPs, grant databases, referral inbox, industry newsletters,
regulator feeds via Market Intelligence.

## Outputs

`opportunities.json` entries, `companies.md` updates, new CRM records,
`daily-report-template.md`.

## Success Metrics

- Qualified opportunities surfaced per week
- Opportunity-to-conversation conversion rate
- Average score of surfaced opportunities over time
- Time from an opportunity appearing publicly to being logged
- Zero missed `Priority`-would-have-scored opportunities, checked
  retrospectively

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Opportunities logged | 10+ |
| Priority or Active scored | 3+ |
| Time-to-log (publication to logged) | Under 24 hours |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Opportunities logged | 40+ |
| Opportunity-to-conversation conversion | 20%+ |
| Priority items handed to Revenue Hunter | 10+ |

## Business Impact

This is the top of the funnel. If this number is low, everything
downstream — Revenue Hunter's pipeline, Sales Director's queue, CEO
Advisor's daily pick — is working with a smaller set of choices, not a
lack of effort further down the chain.
