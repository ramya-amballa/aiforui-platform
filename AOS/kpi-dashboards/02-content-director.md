# KPI Dashboard — Content Director

## Inputs

Market Intelligence's regulatory triggers, CRM patterns, Sales
Director client questions, Product Manager launch notifications.

## Outputs

`content-brief-template.md` entries, `content-calendar-template.md`
updates, published LinkedIn posts, newsletter issues, website updates.

## Success Metrics

- Every published piece traceable to one of the five objectives, no
  exceptions
- Leads attributable to specific content pieces
- Newsletter subscriber growth month over month
- Organic search traffic growth to Insights and Resources pages
- Time from trigger to published content

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Pieces published | 2-3 |
| Pieces with a clear objective tag | 100% |
| Trigger-to-publish time | Under 5 business days |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Pieces published | 8-12 |
| Leads attributable to content | 3+ |
| Newsletter subscriber growth | 5%+ |
| Organic traffic growth (Insights/Resources) | 5%+ |

## Business Impact

Content is the practice's authority signal and its lead-generation
surface outside direct outreach. A gap here means Sales Director and
Revenue Hunter are working a smaller, colder set of relationships,
because nothing new is pulling people toward the practice on its own.
