# KPI Dashboard — Revenue Hunter

## Inputs

Opportunity Hunter's `Priority` items, Product Manager's 30+-scored
candidates, CRM upsell signals, Sales Director stage changes.

## Outputs

`pipeline.json` entries and band/stage updates,
`revenue-dashboard-template.md`.

## Success Metrics

- Total weighted pipeline value (expected revenue x probability)
- Pipeline value by revenue type
- Time from item entering the pipeline to a pursue/defer/drop decision
- Percentage of Priority-tier items that actually convert
- Effort-adjusted return: value delivered per hour of pursuit

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| New pipeline items scored | 100% within 24 hours of entering |
| Priority-band items with a `nextAction` assigned | 100% |

## Monthly Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Priority-tier conversion rate | 30%+ |
| Pipeline concentration in any single revenue type | Under 60% (no single type carrying the whole business) |
| Total weighted pipeline value | Growing month over month |

## Business Impact

Revenue Hunter is the practice's single source of truth for "what is
the business actually worth right now, and how confident are we." The
weighted pipeline value feeds directly into the Revenue Forecasting
Engine and the CEO Dashboard's overall health read.
