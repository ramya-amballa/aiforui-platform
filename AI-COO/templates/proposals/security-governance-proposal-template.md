## Proposed Approach

Run through OPERA, adapted to security governance:

- **Opportunity** — confirm the specific standard or assessment driving
  the work (e.g. ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST CSF) and what "ready" means for
  it
- **People** — assign control ownership at the level where the control
  is actually operated
- **Evaluation** — assess current controls against the target
  baseline, separating documented policy from operating practice
- **Response** — prioritise remediation by the categories with the
  greatest gap to the required level
- **Assurance** — prepare the evidence structure the assessment will
  actually require
