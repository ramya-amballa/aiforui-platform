## Proposed Approach

Run through OPERA, adapted to a technology risk assurance function:

- **Opportunity** — confirm the specific risk decisions the function
  needs to make and who currently makes them
- **People** — assign control ownership at the level where the
  control is actually operated, not where it is reported
- **Evaluation** — reset the risk taxonomy and control library against
  actual exposure
- **Response** — restate risk appetite in language engineering and
  business teams can apply
- **Assurance** — build an assurance cycle that produces evidence
  ahead of audit, not reconstructed after it
