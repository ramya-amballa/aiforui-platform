## Proposed Approach

Run through OPERA across the full scope, sequenced by risk exposure
rather than by domain:

- **Opportunity** — map the real decisions in scope across every
  domain involved, and who owns the business outcome for each
- **People** — one accountability model spanning all domains in scope,
  not separate ownership structures per domain
- **Evaluation** — one consistent risk model calibrated across domains
- **Response** — a single decision and approval structure, so a
  governance decision doesn't require three different processes
  depending on which domain it touches
- **Assurance** — one evidence trail and one reporting cadence to the
  board, covering the full scope
