## Proposed Approach

Fractional engagements run the same OPERA discipline as a project, on
a recurring cadence instead of a fixed end date:

- **Opportunity** — a standing view of AI use cases and governance
  decisions as they arise, not a one-time inventory
- **People** — ongoing ownership and accountability decisions as roles
  and use cases change
- **Evaluation** — recurring risk review, not a single assessment
- **Response** — governance decisions made and documented as they come
  up, at board pace
- **Assurance** — a standing reporting cadence to the board or
  relevant committee
